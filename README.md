# 🚌 UniTransit – Smart University Transport Tracking System

**UniTransit** is a **smart university transport tracking system** developed as a **Final Year Project** for **educational purposes** under university supervision.

It provides:

- 🚍 **Real-time bus locations**
- 🗺 **Route visualization**
- 🕒 **Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA)**

Built with **Flutter & Firebase** and integrated with **Google Maps Platform**, UniTransit improves **efficiency, safety, and convenience** for students and staff across **multi-campus universities**.

## 📌 Project Overview

UniTransit addresses transportation challenges such as:

- ❌ Uncertain bus arrival times  
- ❌ Long waiting times  
- ❌ Route confusion (Boys/Girls/Staff)  
- ❌ Safety concerns  

By providing **real-time GPS tracking**, digital timetables, and route categorization, it enhances the commuting experience.

---

## 🧠 System Architecture

### 🚍 Driver App (Tracker)
- Live GPS location broadcasting  
- Select route & start/stop trip

### 🎓 Student App (Viewer)
- View live bus location  
- Access schedules & route maps  
- ETA calculation

### 🧑‍💼 Admin Panel (Web)
- Manage buses & routes  
- Update schedules  
- Control driver access
